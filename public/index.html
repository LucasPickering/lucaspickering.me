About Me

I like doing just about anything with computers: web development (frontend & backend), networks, compilers, game development, systems programming, robotics, and building keyboards. I graduated from Northeastern University in 2019 and now live in the Boston area.

Languages

Most of my experience is in JavaScript/TypeScript and Python, but my favorite language, by far, is Rust. Rust is awesome. I'm very much still learning it, but its semantics, design patterns, safety guarantees, and ecosystem make it a fantastic language. I also like functional languages (I have experience with Racket and OCaml) because they're very easy to reason about, and also generally quite neat-o.
